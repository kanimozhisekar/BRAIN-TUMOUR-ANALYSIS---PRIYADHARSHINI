About Brain Tumor Analysis

This AI-powered system uses a Vision Transformer (ViT) to analyze MRI brain scans and detect tumors.

Features

  • AI Detection: Identifies glioma, meningioma, pituitary tumors, or no tumor
  • Tumor Localization: Uses Grad-CAM to highlight suspicious regions
  • Treatment Suggestions: Evidence-based guidance (no prognosis)
  • PDF Reports: Download detailed analysis with images

Technology

Model: Vision Transformer (ViT-B-16)

Visualization: Grad-CAM for tumor highlighting

Localization: Automated lobe and hemisphere estimation
